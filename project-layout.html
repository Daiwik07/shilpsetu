Page Overview

Home

Introduces the project and highlights the cultural importance of handicrafts.

Learning

Explains the knowledge gained through research about artisans and crafts.

Timeline

Shows the historical journey of Indian handicrafts from ancient times.

Artisan Stories

Shares inspiring stories about the lives and struggles of artisans.

Support

Visitors can support artisans through awareness forms and initiatives.

Help & FAQ

Provides answers to common questions and contact information.
