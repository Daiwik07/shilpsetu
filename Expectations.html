Expectations

Our expectations for this project are to create a user-friendly and visually appealing website that effectively showcases the
unique products offered by ShilpSetu. We aim to provide an engaging and informative experience for visitors, highlighting the
craftsmanship and cultural significance of the products. Additionally, we hope to increase awareness about ShilpSetu and drive
traffic to the platform, ultimately supporting the artisans and craftsmen who rely on it for their livelihoods.

We also expect to learn and apply various web development skills, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to create a functional
and responsive website. Through this project, we aim to enhance our understanding of user experience design and digital marketing
strategies to effectively promote ShilpSetu and its mission.
