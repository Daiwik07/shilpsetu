Our Team

We are a group of passionate students from Aravali International School, Faridabad, Haryana, India. Our team is dedicated to exploring the rich heritage of Indian arts and crafts and connecting it with the modern world through our project, ShilpSetu.

We are committed to preserving and promoting the traditional arts and crafts of India while embracing innovation and sustainability.
Through our project, we aim to create a bridge between the artisans and the global market, fostering appreciation and support for their work.
