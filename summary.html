Project Summary

Project Idea

Our project focuses on the rich heritage of Indian handicrafts and highlights the creativity and skills of traditional artisans.

Project Goal

The aim of this project is to spread awareness about Indian handicrafts and encourage people to support handmade products.

Research Method

We conducted surveys, collected information from books and websites, and studied different handicrafts from various states of India.

Team Work

Our team worked together to collect data, design the website, create presentations, and organize the research information.

What We Learned

This project helped us understand Indian culture, traditional crafts, research methods, and the importance of preserving heritage.

Importance

Indian handicrafts represent cultural identity and creativity. Promoting them helps protect traditional knowledge and artisans' livelihood.
