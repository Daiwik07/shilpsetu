Surveys

We conducted surveys to gather insights from our target audience, which includes artisans, customers, and stakeholders in the
handicraft industry. The surveys were designed to understand their needs, preferences, and challenges. We used a combination of
online questionnaires and in-person interviews to collect data.

The survey results revealed that artisans face difficulties in reaching a wider market and often lack access to modern tools and
resources. Customers expressed a desire for unique, handcrafted products but also highlighted concerns about pricing and
authenticity. These insights have been instrumental in shaping our project and guiding our efforts to support the artisan community
effectively.
