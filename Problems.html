Problems

Through our research and surveys, we identified several key problems that our project aims to address:

Structural and Economic Issues

  • Unorganised Sector: The handicrafts sector remains largely unorganised, which has hindered its ability to keep pace with modern development.
  • Lack of Modernisation: Many clusters have not updated their production chains, tools, or machinery, resulting in a lack of fresh developmental impetus.
  • Stagnant Output: Due to the lack of modernisation, there has not been an optimum realisation of output within the sector.
  • Scattered Artisan Base: Artisans are often scattered, making it very difficult to build the critical mass required for economies of scale in operations.
  • Inadequate Infrastructure: A lack of world-class infrastructural facilities for production and processing has been a significant barrier to growth.




⚠️ Understanding the challenges artisans face helps us find solutions!
