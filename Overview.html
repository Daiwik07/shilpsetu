Overview

ShilpSetu is an online platform that connects artisans and craftsmen with customers, providing a marketplace for their
handmade products. The platform aims to promote traditional craftsmanship and support local artisans by offering them a
wider audience and increased sales opportunities. ShilpSetu allows artisans to showcase their unique creations, while
customers can discover and purchase authentic handmade products directly from the source.


The platform offers a user-friendly interface where artisans can create their profiles, upload product images, and manage their
inventory. Customers can browse through various categories of products, read artisan stories, and make secure purchases. ShilpSetu also
provides features such as customer reviews and ratings to help customers make informed decisions while supporting the artisans.


By connecting artisans with customers, ShilpSetu not only promotes traditional craftsmanship but also contributes to the economic
empowerment of artisans and the preservation of cultural heritage. The platform fosters a sense of community and encourages sustainable
practices by supporting local artisans and their unique creations.
